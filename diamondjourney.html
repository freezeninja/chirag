The journey of billion-year-old diamonds from mines to you

At Chirag Jewellers - founded in 1972 by the Late Rameshchandra Shah - we choose the best diamonds that reflect your enigma. Our master craftsmen meticulously carve each piece of diamond with a precision matching the standards of excellence. Each jewellery we craft is a mark of quality & honesty. It is no wonder then that today Chirag Jewellers has emerged as the city's foremost authority on the subject of selecting and purchasing exquisite diamond jewellery.

Mining

Priceless treasures of nature - diamonds are formed under volcanic mountains, found on the river bed and at times unearthed from the seashore. But not all match your unparalleled brilliance. We scavenge the best mines of Ghana, Kimberley and Dominoes to choose the stones that emanate the most spell-binding lustre.

Sorting
Rough
Diamonds

Before a raw diamond is processed, it resembles dull pieces of glass. These raw diamonds are taken to processing plants and are sorted into categories according to weight, colour and clarity.

Cleaving & polishing of rough diamonds

After separating rough diamonds to be finished separately, the crucial step of polishing is efficiently executed in our polishing factory in Gujarat.

Inspection
of Polished
diamonds

After the final facets are polished onto the diamond by a brillianteer, the diamonds undergo a meticulous inspection using a 10x loop that qualifies the measure of matching the 4Cs (Colour, Carat, Cut and Clarity.) and thereafter receives its official lab certifications.
